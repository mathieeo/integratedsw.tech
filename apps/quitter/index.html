Quitter

Health & Fitness

Quitter walks you through withdrawal one honest day at a time. Pick what you are quitting (weed, cigarettes, vaping, alcohol, caffeine, sugar, social media, or a custom habit) and get a researched day-by-day timeline: what to expect, what helps, and what to skip. A slip restarts your streak but never erases your lifetime clean days, longest streak, or money saved. The Craving SOS screen gets you through the hard ten minutes with a breathing ball and your own reasons read back to you, a one-tap mood log graphs the climb, milestones from 24 hours to one year get celebrated, and on supported iPhones an on-device AI companion answers questions about your exact day. No account, no ads, no cloud: everything stays on your phone.
