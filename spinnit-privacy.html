Spinnit — Privacy Policy

Integrated Software Technologies Inc. · Last updated July 7, 2026

Short version: Spinnit has no accounts and we run no servers. Your wheels stay on your device. The only data collected comes from the Google AdMob ads that keep the app free — and you can remove ads to stop that entirely.

What Spinnit stores on your device

Spinnit saves the wheels you create, your options and colors, your spin history, and your settings. This is stored on your device (and, if you use iCloud, in your own private iCloud account through Apple). We never receive this information and have no servers that collect it.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Spinnit is free and supported by ads served through Google AdMob. To show ads, AdMob may collect information such as a device advertising identifier, approximate location (derived from IP), and app‑usage and interaction data. This is used to serve and measure ads and to limit ad fraud.

  • On first launch, Spinnit asks for permission to track (Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt). If you decline, you still get ads — they're just less personalized.
  • Google's handling of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy and how Google uses information from partner apps.
  • You can remove ads entirely with the in‑app Remove Ads purchase, which stops ad‑related data collection.

Purchases

Any purchase (such as Remove Ads) is processed by Apple through the App Store. Spinnit never sees your payment details. Apple's handling of that transaction is covered by Apple's privacy policy.

What Spinnit does not do

  • We do not ask you to create an account or sign in.
  • We do not collect your name, email, contacts, or photos.
  • We do not operate any server that receives your personal data.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

Children

Spinnit is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. Because the app shows third‑party ads, we recommend it be used under the guidance of a parent or guardian where appropriate.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
